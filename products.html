Our Products

Over the years Transformational Data insights has developed a variety of high-end tools that can help our clients in the Life Science space and beyond to be more productive, solve specific problems and run their IT solutions more efficiently.

Accurate Antigen

Lookup Tool



  • The Lookup Tool allows one of our clients to employ a remote phone answering company that use the tool to very rapidly check product and customer information. This allows for a more efficent and productive service

Web Based Data & Curation Tool



  • Modified and customized SQL databases that hold all manufactured products and allows cross-functional teams such as R&D, Product Management, Customer Service, Operations and Marketing to work effectively together
